## 1. How can understanding your audience‚Äôs expertise level (tech experts vs. regular folks) shape the way you present technical information?

## 2. What are some strategies to tailor your content to different audience types?

## 9. How do headings and subheadings improve the readability and organization of technical documents?

‚úÖ Why it works: Makes content more inclusive and usable.

‚úÖ Why it works: Increases visibility and retrievability of information.

‚úÖ Why it works: Saves time and effort in document maintenance.

Best Practices for Using Headings & Subheadings
‚úî Use a clear, logical hierarchy (H1 ‚Üí H2 ‚Üí H3).
